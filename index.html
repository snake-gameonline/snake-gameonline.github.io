Frequently Asked Questions

How does the scoring system work?

Points are earned through multiple actions: collecting food (1 point), eliminating opponents (10 points + bonus based on their size), and survival time bonuses. Your final score determines your position on the global leaderboard.

Can I play with friends in a private room?

Yes! Create a private room by clicking the "Create Room" button and share the generated code with up to 10 friends. Private rooms feature customizable settings including map size, food spawn rate, and starting snake length.

What makes Snake IO different from classic Snake?

Snake IO introduces multiplayer competition, power-ups, special abilities, and various game modes while maintaining the core snake-growing mechanics. The addition of real-time interaction with other players creates dynamic and unpredictable gameplay scenarios.
